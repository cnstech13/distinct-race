Who We Are

Distict Race Foundation is a nonprofit organization committed to empowering individuals to discover their purpose, build confidence, and positively impact society.

Through leadership development, mentorship, education, and community outreach, we inspire people to become agents of positive change.

Read More

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To intentionally teach, train, and empower young people to discover their identity, renew their mindset, and live purpose-driven lives through discipleship, mentorship, and impactful community outreach.

Vision

To raise a generation of distinct young people who understand their identity in Christ, walk in purpose, and influence society with excellence and light.

“ But ye are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, an holy nation, a peculiar people; that ye should shew forth the praises of him who hath called you out of darkness into his marvellous light: — 1 Peter 2:9 ”

Values

Integrity, Compassion, Leadership, Excellence, Service.

Our Core Objectives (what we actually do)

  • Teach identity and purpose from a Christ-centered perspective.
  • Train young people in personal growth, leadership, and life skills
  • Mentor and guide youths toward clarity and direction
  • Conduct school outreaches and community impact projects
  • Provide opportunities, exposure, and support systems for growth
  • Raise responsible individuals who positively influence society

Pillars (This is what make us "all-round" but structured)


  1. Identity & Spiritual Growth

    2. Helping young people understand who they are in Christ and grow in their relationship with God.


  2. Mindset & Personal Development

    3. Renewing thinking patterns, building confidence, discipline, and character.


  3. Purpose & Leadership

    4. Guiding young people to discover purpose and develop leadership capacity.


  4. Skills & Opportunities

    5. Equipping them with relevant skills, exposure, and access to opportunities.


  5. Outreach & Social Impact

    6. Reaching schools, communities, and giving back through impactful initiatives.

Our Programs

Purpose Discovery

Helping youths discover their unique gifts and calling through mentorship, leadership development, education, and personal growth, empowering them to become confident, purpose-driven individuals who positively influence their communities and the world.

Leadership Training

We are committed to developing ethical and visionary leaders who serve with excellence, empower others, and work tirelessly to bring lasting positive transformation to their communities, nation, and the world.

Education

We organize engaging workshops, seminars, and mentorship sessions that provide guidance, knowledge, and practical life skills, helping young people discover their potential, develop leadership qualities, and make meaningful contributions to society.

Community Outreach

We touch lives through selfless service and genuine compassion by supporting vulnerable individuals, promoting community development, and creating opportunities that inspire hope, dignity, and lasting positive change.
