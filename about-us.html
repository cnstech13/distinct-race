Who We Are

Distinct Race Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping individuals, especially young people, discover their purpose, embrace their identity, and unlock their full potential. We believe that every person has unique gifts and the ability to make a meaningful difference in their community and beyond.

Through leadership development, mentorship, education, life-skills training, and community outreach, we equip individuals with the knowledge, confidence, and values needed to become responsible, purpose-driven leaders and agents of positive change.

At Distinct Race Foundation, we are committed to inspiring hope, promoting personal growth, and creating opportunities that empower people to transform their lives and contribute to building stronger, more compassionate communities.
